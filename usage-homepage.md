# Homepage

## Setup homepage

Go to `Admin` -> `Apearance` -> `Theme options`,  at the **Page** tab, you can choose what homepage do you want to display.

![](./images/homepage-setup.png)

## Customize homepage

Go to `Admin` -> `Pages` and select the **Homepage 1**
